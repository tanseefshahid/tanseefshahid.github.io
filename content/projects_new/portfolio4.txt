### Single-Image to 3D Reconstruction Pipeline via OpenLRM & Neural Rendering

**Project Overview**
Architected and trained an advanced image-to-3D generative pipeline capable of producing high-fidelity 3D meshes from a single 2D image. The system leverages the OpenLRM (Large Reconstruction Model) framework, supported by a custom synthetic rendering pipeline, to enable real-time 3D asset generation for AR/VR, virtual staging, and gaming environments.

**The Challenge**
Extracting accurate 3D geometry from a single 2D image is an ill-posed problem due to severe depth ambiguity. Initial iterations using traditional multi-view pixel matching and Occupancy Networks struggled with depth resolution. While subsequent NeRF implementations improved visual quality, they required computationally expensive per-scene optimization. The objective was to build a fast, feed-forward network capable of generating accurate, textured 3D topology instantly, without compromising dimensional accuracy.

**Technical Approach & Architecture** 
* **Procedural Synthetic Data Generation:**
    * Built an automated rendering pipeline using **Blender (bpy scripting)** to process a large-scale CAD dataset of furniture (tables, chairs, bookshelves, beds, sofas).
    * Extracted 24 precise 360-degree views per object, optimizing camera rotation, focal length, and illumination to perfectly capture complex surface topography.
    * Integrated **ESRGAN** (Enhanced Super-Resolution GAN) to upscale the rendered images, injecting high-frequency texture details to prevent the 3D model from appearing smoothed or blurred during training.
* **Model R&D and OpenLRM Fine-Tuning:**
    * Transitioned from implicit neural representations (NeRFs) to a transformer-based **OpenLRM** architecture to achieve rapid, feed-forward 3D generation.
    * Fine-tuned the model on the high-resolution synthetic dataset, optimizing the network to predict accurate triplane representations and extract clean, manifold meshes.
* **Metrology & Geometric Evaluation:**
    * Established a rigorous evaluation protocol to measure the structural integrity of the generated meshes against the ground-truth CAD models, focusing on surface normals and point-cloud deviations rather than just 2D visual fidelity.

**Impact & Results**
* **High-Precision Geometry:** Achieved exceptional metrological accuracy on complex furniture topologies, recording an **IoU of 0.80**, a **Chamfer Distance of 0.08**, and a **Normal Consistency of 0.82**.
* **Scalable 3D Production:** Successfully replaced computationally heavy NeRF optimizations with a feed-forward transformer pipeline, drastically reducing inference time and enabling real-time asset generation for spatial computing applications.

**Tools & Technologies**
* **3D Deep Learning:** OpenLRM, NeRF, Occupancy Networks, Triplane Representations
* **Rendering & Augmentation:** Blender (bpy), Objaverse techniques, ESRGAN
* **Languages & Frameworks:** Python, PyTorch, Py3D
